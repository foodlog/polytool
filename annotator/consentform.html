Participants Information

Introduction

The research that you're about to participate in has a very wide scope, but this specific task focuses on improving the quality of crowdsourced image annotations, which will eventually be implemented into Machine Learning models. The primary reason for experimenting on a smaller scale like this, is that it is a great help for us to improve our algorithms, which will help us improve our end product!
For more information about this research or any other queries, feel free to contact davies-crenLE@cardiff.ac.uk.

Important Information

Please read through this information before signing the consent form:

  • Your participation is voluntarily.
  • Pressing the 'Submit' button at the bottom of the page is considered as consent for your participation.
  • You may withdraw from the research at any time and for any reason.
  • You have any given amount of time to consider your participation, you are by no means obligated to participate.
  • None of your personal data will be collected, but your annotations will be collected for further development purposes and will not be presented or published
    (this will be stored for the period of the study, and an additional 5 years after that.)
